Name: The Ancient Wizard's Star

Genre: open world
Characters: wizards, knights, assassins, miners, ninjas, robots, monsters 
Setting: magical world, with things that don’t exist in the real world like wizards,  mythical creatures, and monsters
Movement: walk or run
Loop: walk through the world, fight monsters, get shards, find armor, level up, fight three bosses with two helpers, and light the ancient dimensional portal gate
after the portal gate, there is a big boss, after which you win the first loop
There are respawn points scattered around the world
Collectible: magical shards to level up, weapons, armor - collect in the open world or drops from monsters
Health/energy: starter health is 10, starter energy is also 10 and you have to level up to gain more energy and health. If you die, you respawn
Interaction: you can buy stuff from certain people, talk to random npcs, there’s also a boss animation, if you want to talk to npcs you just have to say the sentence in real life, and you can also move light objects
